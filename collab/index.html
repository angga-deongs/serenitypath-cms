Advances in science and medicine—including new diagnostics, protocols, treatments, and knowledge—should benefit everyone. Today, there's a vast delta between those who have access to high-quality, up-to-date medical care and those who need it most. We hope to change that.

The Atria Health Collaborative is a nonprofit organization that aims to help eradicate medical inequity locally, nationally, and globally. We bring together doctors, researchers, and public health experts from leading institutions to guide our investment in and promotion of proven interventions and promising new approaches to preventable and reversible diseases.

Leveraging the expertise of the Atria Academy of Science & Medicine fellows and Atria Institute’s in-house team, we focus on the most pressing diseases and biggest killers faced by medically underserved communities around the world. We will freely share expert knowledge to combat the reversible and preventable diseases that needlessly cause the most suffering around the world.

Delivering life-changing
knowledge and care.

01

Making a global impact

By focusing our energy on medically underserved communities, we target locations with meaningful gaps in services, where we’re uniquely positioned to make a difference. We will work tirelessly to scale our evidence-based clinical methods to make them widely available.

02

Expanding medical knowledge

The Atria Academy of Science & Medicine will provide a channel to capture and transfer knowledge, and enable us to invest in and promote life-saving interventions. We will share our learnings with the medical community, policymakers, and the public, who can replicate and build on our progress.

03

Measuring our momentum

Our impact will be measured through transparent reporting. We target our efforts in areas where we hope to solve meaningful gaps in services, where we are uniquely positioned to make a difference, and where we can observe and track our impact.
